A Dream Wedding Dress...

By David Benioff & D. B. Weiss

Whether you've been dreaming of your wedding dress since you were five and know the exact specifications you want for it down to the last sequin, or you've never thought about it until your future spouse's mother nagged, "You still haven't got your gown," we've got you covered on how to choose a wedding dress. Finding your dream wedding dress can be stressful. After all, you've likely never tried one on before, and it's the most expensive garment many women will ever own, so it can feel like the pressure is definitely on while wedding dress shopping. It may also feel like there's a whole new language to decode—tulle, A-line, fit-and-flare, organza, sheath, etc.

Boho Wedding Decor Ideas

By Sully Hagedorn-Alvarez

Inspired by nature and the bohemian romantic free spirit, boho-chic wedding decor is most brides’ favorite wedding theme. Soft and romantic with a poetic vibe and just slightly opulent, the bohemian wedding decor staples include earthy tones, natural elements, and airy materials inspired by nature and the outdoors. When you think bohemian theme think of elements like tassels, macrame, dream catchers paired with green leaves, wildflowers, pampas grass (what is a boho wedding without it!), dried palm leaves, etc., all in an earthy color palette with muted hues. Use them to fill beautiful vases, in hanging wedding centerpieces, on your wedding arch, literally everywhere, and you will surely end up with the whimsical wedding decor of your dreams.
